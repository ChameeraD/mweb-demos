How to vote using dummy Electronic Voting Machine (EVM) 101

Follow the below sample dummy EVM 101 voting guide

Vote in Person using dummy Electronic Voting Machine (EVM)

  1. EVM stands for Electronic Voting Machine.
  2. It consists of two Units – a Control Unit and a Balloting Unit – joined by a five-meter cable.
  3. The Control Unit is with the Presiding Officer or a Polling Officer and the Balloting Unit is placed inside the voting compartment.
  4. Instead of issuing a ballot paper, the Polling Officer in-charge of the Control Unit will press the Ballot Button.

Vote using dummy Postal Ballot

A system of voting in which people send their votes by post when they are not present at the polling station where they are enrolled as a voter. Below are the list of voters who are entitled to vote by ballot papers:
  • service voters;
  • special voters;
  • the wives of persons referred to in clauses (a) and (b) above, who are originally residence with them,
  • electors subjected to preventive detention;
  • voters on election duty; and
  • Notified voters.
