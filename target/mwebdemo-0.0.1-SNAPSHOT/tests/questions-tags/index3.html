Dummy Electronic Voting Machine (EVM) 101 Questions

What is Electronic Voting Machine (EVM)

  1. EVM stands for Electronic Voting Machine.
  2. It consists of two Units – a Control Unit and a Balloting Unit – joined by a five-meter cable.
  3. The Control Unit is with the Presiding Officer or a Polling Officer and the Balloting Unit is placed inside the voting compartment.
  4. Instead of issuing a ballot paper, the Polling Officer in-charge of the Control Unit will press the Ballot Button.
  5. This will enable the voter to cast his vote by pressing the blue button on the Balloting Unit against the candidate and symbol of his choice.

What is Electors Photo Identity Card (EPIC)?

EPIC stands for Electors Photo Identity Card commonly known as Voter ID. The Indian voter ID card is an identity document issued by the Election Commission of India which primarily serves as an identity proof for Indian citizens while casting votes in the country's state and national elections.
